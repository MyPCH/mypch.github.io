About


The consortium

The project consortium consists of Diabetes Services (Denmark) and OwnYourData (Austria).

Both partners are selected as MyData Operators 2020.



Diabetes Services

Diabetes Services is an IT service company and social-enterprise that is building a human-centric digital ecosystem of data using services for diabetes- and lifestyle management for better health and quality of life.
Read more on website: Diabetes Services.

OwnYourData

OwnYourData is a non-profit association that helps you achieve unrestricted access to your data for your benefit.
Read more on website: OwnYourData.

Supported by

MyData Global

MyData Global is an award-winning international non-profit organisation. The purpose of MyData Global is to empower individuals by improving their right to self-determination regarding their personal data. It is based on the MyData Declaration.
Read more on website: MyData Global.

Steno Diabetes Center Copenhagen

Steno Diabetes Center Copenhagen (SDCC) is the largest diabetes clinic in Scandinavia. Every year SDCC treats more than 9,000 people with diabetes from the Capital Region.
Read more on website: Steno Diabetes Center Copenhagen.

Danish Diabetes Association

The Danish Diabetes Association is a member-organisation that supports people with diabetes. With approximately 90,000 members the organisation is one the largest patient associations in Denmark.
Read more on website: The Danish Diabetes Association.
